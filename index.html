I AM A PERSON FROM NEW YORK

Foodie/Brewsie

I'm not a competitive eater but I play one on Instagram. You can also find me exploring different beer gardens and brew houses exploring wonderful craft beer. One of the things that I find to be less and less common are people that are willing try try new and exciting foods. I think the reason why I'm such an adventurous eating is because my background and heritage is so diverse, with influences of African, Indian, Spanish, Dutch and Native American cultures playing a big part.

Outdoorsy

I think the more I dive into the tech space the more I look to escape into the wild. I'm an avid bike riding, hiker, camper, fisherman and all-around city explorer. I enjoy outdoor sports year-round and dream of my next weekend getaway. When I'm not outside, I'm planning on going outside.

Traveler

Trip planning is equal parts therapeutic, educational and just plain ol' fun. I enjoy learning about history, new cultures, food, and geography and I feel that the best way is to go out and (sometimes) get lost in a different place or in the place you've known all your life but see it through a different lense.

People Person

I'm super accessible and open. One of my passions in life is connecting with new people and learning about their stories and their dreams and see how I can help them. I've always had a skill for communication and I'm usually the weirdo on the street to that sparks up a conversation with a total stranger (I can imagine my parents cringing at me talking to strangers as a kid). Whether it's debugging or grabbing drinks (why not both?), the human connection matters.

I ENJOY CREATING THINGS THAT SOLVE EVERY-DAY PROBLEMS

DiffRent

DiffRent is an inclusive, lightweight & easy-to-use platform for landlords and tenants to communicate. Whether you are a landlord with multiple apartments or a tenant that needs some work done, our ticketing system is easy to use for any issues that might arise in an apartment. Users can schedule appointments for site-visits & create and store messages within the app.
DiffRent was built using Javascript, React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, HTML & CSS.

HikeMe!

HikeMe! helps outdoor enthusiasts find a hiking trail nearest them. This mobile friendly app gives you the ability to filter a list of hiking trails by distance & difficulty. Users of HikeMe! will get the name, image, rating, location & description of a trail that interests them so they can get out and surround themselves with nature in no-time.
HikeMe! was built by developers that love the outdoors and using technologies such as React, HTML, CSS and the Hiking Project API
